MAIN INFORMATION ABOUT STRAY KIDS

Stray Kids primarily self-produce its recordings; the main production team is named 3Racha and consists of Bang Chan, Changbin, and Han, and the other members frequently participate in songwriting. Their last five releases (Noeasy-2021; Oddinary, Maxident-2022; 5-Star, Rock-Star-2023; Ate-2024) peaked at number one on the US Billb ntered the UK Albums Chart, making them the first band to debut at the top of Billboard 200 with their first-five-charted albums. The KMCA certified 5-Star five-million in album sales, making Stray Kids the third group to achieve this in Korea. As of October 2022, Stray Kids has sold over 10 million albums, both Korean and Japanese releases. In 2023, the band appeared on Time's list of Next Generation Leaders.
