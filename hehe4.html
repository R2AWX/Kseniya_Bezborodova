THE BEST PERFOMANCE

At the 2020 Mnet Asian Music Awards, it was announced Stray Kids would join Ateez and The Boyz on the inaugural season of Kingdom: Legendary War, a Mnet boy-group competition show; BtoB, iKon and SF9 were later also confirmed as participants. On May 28, 2021, Stray Kids released a song titled "Wolfgang" for the final round of the competition, which charted at number 138 on the main Gaon Digital Chart, the band's first appearance on that chart. The band won the program on June 3, earning them their own reality show and a Kingdom Week special show.
